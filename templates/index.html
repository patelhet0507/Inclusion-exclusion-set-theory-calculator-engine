Formula help

Union of 2: |A ∪ B| = |A| + |B| - |A ∩ B|

Union of 3: |A ∪ B ∪ C| = |A| + |B| + |C| - |A ∩ B| - |A ∩ C| - |B ∩ C| + |A ∩ B ∩ C|

Intersection: |A ∩ B| = |A| + |B| - |A ∪ B|

Difference: |A - B| = |A| - |A ∩ B|

Example: If |A|=20, |B|=15 and |A ∪ B|=25, then |A ∩ B| = 20 + 15 - 25 = 10
